Our Philosophy

Craft & Conviviality

At Vine & Grain, we celebrate the marriage of exceptional wines and thoughtfully prepared cuisine. Our bistro brings together the warmth of neighborhood dining with the sophistication of a wine bar.

Founded in 2015, we've cultivated relationships with small vineyards and local producers to bring you authentic flavors with a modern twist. Our seasonal menus reflect the best of each harvest, paired with an ever-evolving wine list curated by our sommelier.

Our Collection

The Wine Cellar

Our cellar houses an ever-changing selection of over 150 wines, with a focus on small producers and sustainable viticulture. From bold reds to crisp whites and everything between, our sommelier has crafted a list that complements our menu while offering discoveries for every palate.

Join us for weekly wine tastings or book a private guided tasting for your group.
